## Overview

Borneo Jungle Adventure is an interactive text-based adventure game that takes you deep into the wonders of the jungle. Face challenges, answer riddles, and make choices that will determine your fate in this vibrant adventure!

- **Riddles**: Answer challenging riddles posed by the wise hornbill.
- **Health System**: Maintain your health with decisions that impact your journey.
- **Interactive Choices**: Every decision can lead you to a different outcome!

## Getting Started

1. Open a browser with developer tools or use any JavaScript editor.
2. Copy and paste the game code into the console or script of your HTML file.
3. Run the code to start your adventure!

## Rules of the Game

- Start by entering your name to begin your journey.
- Choose between the jungle or river path.
- Make choices wisely as they will affect your health and the outcome of your adventure.
- You can lose hearts based on decisions, so choose wisely!

## Endgame

Your adventure can end in two ways:

- You successfully complete your journey!
- You run out of health and lose the game.
